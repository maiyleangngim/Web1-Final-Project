BELTEI International University (BIU) is a leading private institution in Cambodia, widely recognized for its high standards of discipline and quality education. Founded by H.E. Ly Chheng, the university operates multiple modern campuses across Phnom Penh. BIU is unique for its "BELTEI Formula"—a specialized curriculum that blends national standards with international perspectives. The university offers a wide range of programs across 12 distinct faculties, delivered in both Khmer and English. With a strong focus on International Relations, Law, and Digital Technology, BIU prepares students for the competitive global market through its diverse study modes including on-campus, online, and blended learning.
