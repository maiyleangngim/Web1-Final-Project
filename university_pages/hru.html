Human Resource University (HRU) was established in 1998 as the Institute of Human Resource Development (IHRD) before being promoted to a full university in 2005. Located in the heart of Phnom Penh, HRU is dedicated to producing high-quality human resources through a blend of academic research and practical training. With a mission to empower students with ethical leadership and professional skills, HRU has become one of Cambodia's most popular choices for Business and Social Science studies. The university is fully recognized by the Accreditation Committee of Cambodia (ACC) and maintains strong partnerships with international institutions to ensure its curriculum meets global standards and market demands.
