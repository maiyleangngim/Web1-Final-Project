The National University of Management (NUM) is Cambodia’s leading university for business, management, economics, law, and digital economy studies. Located in Phnom Penh, NUM offers bachelor’s, master’s, and doctoral programs with strong international partnerships and a focus on research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
