The University of Puthisastra (UP) is a premier private university in Cambodia, recognized for its heavy focus on Health Sciences and Digital Technology. Since its founding in 2007, UP has committed to international standards, partnering with global institutions to provide students with world-class research opportunities and hands-on clinical experience. With a mission to produce high-caliber health professionals and tech innovators, UP combines a rigorous academic curriculum with modern laboratories and a strong emphasis on English proficiency and soft skills.
