Excellence in Education
on the Cambodian Coast

Life University (LU) is a private Christian university located in the heart of Sihanoukville. Established in 2006, the university is committed to providing high-quality education integrated with moral and ethical values. Ranked 42nd nationally, Life University is a key educational hub for the coastal region, offering diverse programs in Health Sciences, Business, and Technology. With its modern campus in Sangkat 2, LU provides students with a supportive community and global perspectives through its international leadership and partnerships, preparing graduates to serve with professional competence and integrity.
