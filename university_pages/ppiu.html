Phnom Penh International University (PPIU) is one of the leading private higher education institutions in Cambodia. Established in 2002, PPIU is dedicated to providing high-quality education that meets both national and international standards. With a strong emphasis on digital education, the university prepares students for the evolving global job market. Located in the heart of the city, our 15-storey campus features modern facilities including specialized computer labs, audio labs for language learning, and a comprehensive research library designed to foster academic excellence and critical thinking.
