Leading institution dedicated to research, higher education, and national development

The Royal Academy of Cambodia’s primary mission is to advance the study of both social and natural sciences, provide research insights for the Royal Government, and deliver high-quality graduate education—from master’s to doctoral programs—to support national development grounded in knowledge and vision.
