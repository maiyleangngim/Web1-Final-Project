A leading private university in Cambodia focused on quality education

The University of Cambodia is a private university that offers a range of degrees including Associate’s, Bachelor’s, Master’s, and Doctoral programs. All Colleges and Schools are officially recognized by the Royal Government of Cambodia (RGC), the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport (MoEYS), and the Accreditation Committee of Cambodia (ACC). In addition, UC is also a member of the Cambodian Higher Education Association (CHEA).
