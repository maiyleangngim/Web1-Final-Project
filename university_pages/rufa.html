The Royal University of Fine Arts is a national university with the task of training and researching in the arts and culture with quality, competence, clear skills, ethics, and entrepreneurship, based on the national policy on the cultural sector of the Ministry of Culture and Fine Arts and the policy on the Higher Education Vision 2030 of the Ministry of Education, Youth, and Sports.
