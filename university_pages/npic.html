Excellence and Innovation

The National Polytechnic Institute of Cambodia (NPIC) was established in 2005 through a partnership between the Ministry of Labour and Vocational Training and the Government of the Republic of Korea. Located in Phum Prey Popel, NPIC is Cambodia's premier center for technical and vocational education and training (TVET). The institute is designed to bridge the skills gap in the industrial sector by providing high-level technical training using state-of-the-art machinery and Korean-standard laboratory equipment. NPIC focuses on "learning by doing," ensuring that every student graduates with the practical expertise required by modern factories and engineering firms.
