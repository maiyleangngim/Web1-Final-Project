A Global Tech Hub
Nestled in Nature

Kirirom Institute of Technology (KIT) offers a unique "Living and Learning" experience located atop Kirirom Mountain National Park. Established in 2014, KIT is a private institution that pioneers an AI-focused education delivered entirely in English. Unlike urban universities, KIT provides a serene, forest-based environment that fosters deep focus and innovation. Our curriculum emphasizes Project-Based Learning (PBL) and internship opportunities, preparing students to become global leaders in the digital age while living in a vibrant, international community of students from Cambodia, Japan, and beyond.
