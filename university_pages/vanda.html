Vanda Institute, established in 1997 as the Vanda Accounting Center, is Cambodia's premier institution for business and financial education. With a laser focus on Accounting, Auditing, and Taxation, Vanda has earned a reputation for excellence that makes its graduates highly sought after by the "Big Four" auditing firms and major banks. Operating across branches in Phnom Penh, Siem Reap, and Battambang, Vanda combines rigorous technical training with practical application. The institute's mission is to meet high-quality market demands through the use of digital technology and research, ensuring that every student becomes a high-caliber professional ready to drive Cambodia's economic growth.
