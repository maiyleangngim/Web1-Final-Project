Cambodia's Leading Public
University for Law and Business

The Royal University of Law and Economics (RULE) is the first higher education institution in Cambodia, originally founded in 1948. As a premier public university, RULE is the national leader in cultivating legal experts, economists, and business professionals. RULE is particularly famous for its International Dual Degree programs in partnership with prestigious European universities. By combining traditional academic rigor with modern international cooperation, RULE empowers students to become ethical leaders and critical thinkers capable of navigating the complexities of both local and global markets.
