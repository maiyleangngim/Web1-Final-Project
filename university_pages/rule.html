At the American University of Phnom Penh (AUPP), we don’t just teach – we empower builders of a brighter tomorrow. Here at AUPP, we ignite transformation through American-style, holistic education rooted in culturally diverse values – awakening active learners to become critical thinkers, bold innovators, ethical leaders, and responsible global citizens. With quality-assured outcome-based curriculum, we empower active, experiential, technology-enhanced learning: hands-on projects, industry immersion, AI-robotics-cybersecurity labs, work-based challenges, and entrepreneurial ventures – fostering courage, integrity, lifelong curiosity, and real-world impact.
