IIC University of Technology (IIC) is a prominent private higher education institution in Cambodia, officially evolved into a full university in 2008. Located in the Chbar Ampov district, IIC is dedicated to providing accessible, high-quality education that bridges the gap between academic theory and labor market needs. The university is widely recognized for its flexible credit-based system and strong emphasis on Business, Law, and Information Technology. With a mission to foster human resource development, IIC offers a supportive learning environment featuring modern air-conditioned classrooms and international partnerships that provide students with global exchange and scholarship opportunities.
